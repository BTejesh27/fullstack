About the Institute

Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKREC), established in 1980, is a self-financed academic institution of co-education striving to provide a high-quality technical education to engineering aspirants. Being one of the premier and well-established technical institutions of the country, it continues to render service to the nation and the world at large with its alumni holding highly prestigious positions and making substantial contributions.
The president of the institution Sri. S. Prasada Raju carries forward the vision of his father Late Sri Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju, a philanthropist, driving the institution on the academic path of excellence. The college is replete with lush coconut trees spanning across a plot area of 30 acres at Chinna Amiram, Bhimavaram, on the Bhimavaram- Vijayawada state highway and it is also well-connected to all other places by road and rail transport.

ABOUT AICTE IDEALAB

The AICTE IDEALAB at SRKR Engineering College has been established in the year 2022 to transform the engineering education imparted in the institution to promote multi- disciplinary approach and learning by doing applying innovation and research. IDEA (Idea, Development, Evaluation & Application) Lab is funded by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. that creates a platform for innovation and research to students and faculty with hands-on experience with the latest equipment like 3D Printing, FAB Lab, and product design tools in order to develop new projects and offer live internships with industry support. IDEA Lab creates the ecosystem for transforming ideation to Prototype or Product.
More Details Contact Us



ABOUT COMPUTER SCIENCE & DESIGN

The Computer Science & Design (CSD) branch started at SRKREC from 2021 to impart quality Computer Science education with a focus on Software Development, UI/UX Design, Industry 4.0 and Coding. The branch offers a 4 year B.Tech in Computer Science & Design with 60 seats and has active clubs namely Software Development Club, Startup Club, IEI Student Chapter, Swecha Learning Centre. CSD has adopted the new National Education Policy (NEP) promoting innovation and entrepreneurship among its students imparting 21st Century Skills creating future ready problem solvers and better citizens.

OBJECTIVES OF THE FDP


  • To create feasible and user-focused solutions to complex problems in the design.
  • To familiarize the participants with cutting-edge viewpoints, notions, principles, and solutions for implementing innovation-centered endeavors through the application of design thinking principles.
  • To follow a practical hands-on approach to the development of a new product, new service, or process.
  • To understand the definition and principles of UI/UX Design in order to design with intention.
  • To understand the definition and principles of UI/UX Design in order to design with intention.
  • To achieve a deep understanding of the entire life-cycle of design—the process, purpose, and tools.
  • To discover the industry-standard tools and specific project deliverables in UI/UX.
